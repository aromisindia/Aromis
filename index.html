Our Story

The Company Behind the Scent

At Aromis, we believe scent defines how a space is felt and remembered. We create reliable, long-lasting fragrance solutions making scenting effortless, consistent and cost-effective.

About Aromis

Aromis is a leading provider of scenting solutions in India, specializing in automatic diffuser machines, commercial air fresheners, and fragrance solutions for hotels, offices, retail stores, and clinics.

We supply scent machines, HVAC diffuser systems, and long-lasting air fragrance solutions that enhance customer experience, brand identity, and ambient scenting across premium spaces.

Our diffuser machines and air freshener are cost-effective, low-maintenance, and built for continuous performance, making them ideal for hospitality, corporate, and commercial environments.

Based in Ahmedabad, Aromis delivers pan-India scent solutions, offering installation, service, and customized fragrance strategies trusted by growing hotel and business networks.

About the Founder

Aromis was founded by Aditya Gupta, a graduate of Christ University, Bangalore, with a simple belief that how a space feels matters just as much as how it looks.

He noticed that most hotels and commercial spaces relied on basic air fresheners and diffuser machines that lacked consistency and identity. What was missing wasn't just fragrance, but a deeper sensory experience that people could connect with and remember.

With that thought, Aromis was built to bring more meaning into spaces through reliable, long-lasting scent marketing solutions that quietly enhance mood, comfort, and perception.

Today, as Aromis works with renowned hotel groups like Ramada, Regenta, and Pride Group, the vision remains the same — to create spaces that leave a lasting impression, not through what people see, but through what they feel.

Why It Matters

Scent Is the Most Powerful Brand Signal You're Not Using

Scent plays a powerful role in shaping customer experience, mood, and memory, especially in hotels, offices, and retail spaces. A well-designed ambient scenting strategy can influence how people perceive your brand and how long they choose to stay.

With the right scent marketing solution, diffuser machines, and air freshener systems, businesses can create a consistent and inviting atmosphere that enhances comfort and leaves a lasting impression.

For hotels and commercial spaces, professional fragrance solutions are no longer optional — they are essential for building brand identity, customer retention, and premium experiences.

Automatic Scent Machine (400ml)

Our flagship fragrance diffuser, designed for hotels and commercial environments that demand consistent, high-quality scenting.

The Aromis Automatic Scent Machine uses advanced diffusion technology to disperse ultra-fine fragrance evenly across spaces ranging from 1,500 to 2,000 sq ft — delivering a seamless fragrance experience without noise or manual effort.

App-controlled and fully programmable, this scent machine is a practical and cost-effective odour control solution for hotel lobbies, corridors, washrooms and commercial spaces across India.

  • App-controlled operation — adjust timing, intensity, and schedules remotely
  • Cold-air diffusion technology — no heat, no residue, no fragrance degradation
  • Wide coverage (1,500–2,000 sq ft) — ideal for hotel lobbies, corridors, showrooms, and offices
  • Low maintenance — refill required every 30–60 days depending on usage

Automatic Scent Machine (800ml)

Built for large commercial environments, the Aromis 800ml Automatic Scent Machine delivers powerful, consistent fragrance across expansive areas ranging from 6,000 to 8,000 sq ft.

Designed for seamless integration, it can be used as a standalone unit or connected directly to HVAC systems for uniform scent distribution across entire floors.

Using advanced cold-air diffusion technology, this system ensures even fragrance coverage without heat, residue, or manual intervention — making it ideal for premium scenting in high-footfall spaces.

  • Wide coverage (6,000–8,000 sq ft) — ideal for large lobbies, banquet halls, retail floors, and open commercial areas
  • HVAC integration compatible — connects directly to air conditioning systems for centralized scent diffusion
  • Cold-air diffusion technology — no heat, no residue, no fragrance degradation
  • App-controlled system — manage timing, intensity, and schedules remotely
  • High-capacity 800ml system — longer runtime with fewer refills
  • Engineered for uniform fragrance distribution across large spaces
